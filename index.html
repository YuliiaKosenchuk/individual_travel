Your Questions

How does an individual tour differ from a group tour?

In an individual tour, there is maximum emphasis on privacy, you do not join a group of people. You can stay where you like the most and get more vivid impressions.

How to book a tour?

Leave a request on the website and we will write to you on WhatsApp/Telegram/Viber. Or take the initiative and write to us first at +380990002355 in any messenger convenient for you :)

Can I change the duration of the tour?

Of course, we can both shorten and lengthen the duration of the tour, taking into account your personal wishes.

What to take from things?

We'll give you a detailed list of things in a special guide, but you'll need to take both warm clothes (especially for the Rainbow Mountains - including sweaters and light jackets), and pants/shirts/dresses/T-shirts for other locations.
